Human-Centered Design

Designing systems and solutions that put people first, ensuring technology serves human needs and aspirations.

Why This Matters Now

Tech-First Design Crisis

AI systems deployed with no human factors research—interfaces optimized for engagement, not wellbeing.

Accessibility Emergency

98% of websites fail basic accessibility standards—new AI tools exclude billions from economic participation.

Diminished Agency

Black-box AI decisions remove human control—algorithmic recommendations replace autonomous choice.

Scale Without Humanity

Platforms prioritize metrics over meaning—billions served, zero understanding of human needs.

Our Focus Areas

  • Human-AI Interaction Design — Interface patterns restoring control, clarity, and dignity
  • Universal Accessibility — AI tools usable by people with diverse cognitive and physical abilities
  • Participatory Co-Design — Communities as collaborators, not data sources or consumers
  • Cognitive Ergonomics — Systems aligned with human attention, memory, and decision-making
  • Cultural Sensitivity — Design respecting diverse values, norms, and contexts globally
  • Ethical Experience Design — Interfaces promoting autonomy over manipulation
  • Wellbeing-Centered Metrics — Success measured by human flourishing, not engagement time
