About Us

Who We Are

LASTHUMAN Foundation is an independent global think tank and nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting, redefining and advancing the role of humanity in the age of Artificial Intelligence.

We operate at the intersection of technology, ethics, social impact and future governance, addressing one of the most critical questions of our time. We believe the future doesn't just happen — it's built by those who dare to imagine it.

Whether you're a researcher, developer, policymaker, artist, or simply someone who cares about our collective future — your voice matters. Together, we can ensure AI serves humanity's highest aspirations.

Our Mission

Building a Human-Centered Future

Our mission is to build a future where AI and advanced technologies are developed

for humans with humans around humans

This is not just a vision — it's a commitment that requires action from all of us. Every contribution, every voice, every idea brings us closer to a future where technology amplifies our humanity rather than diminishing it.

What We Do

Our Areas of Focus

We bring together experts from AI, data science, cybersecurity, public policy, philosophy, education and social innovation. Join a global community of thinkers and doers who refuse to leave the future to chance.

Responsible AI Strategies

Shaping ethical frameworks and guidelines for AI development that prioritize human values and societal benefit. Help us create standards that protect what makes us human.

Technology Policy

Influencing international technology policy through research, advocacy and collaboration with policymakers worldwide. Your expertise can shape the laws that govern our technological future.

Human-Centered Design

Designing systems and solutions that put people first, ensuring technology serves human needs and aspirations. Bring your creative vision to projects that truly matter.

Digital Transformation Support

Supporting individuals and communities navigating digital transformation with education, resources and guidance. Share your knowledge and empower others to thrive in the digital age.

AI Safety Research

Investigating AI alignment, robustness and safety mechanisms to ensure artificial intelligence remains beneficial and controllable. Contribute to research that safeguards humanity's future.

Social Impact Initiatives

Developing programs that address digital divide, algorithmic bias, and ensure equitable access to AI benefits across all communities. Help build a more inclusive technological future.
Our Vision

Sustainable, Ethical & Inclusive

Through research, policy initiatives, education programs, artistic and cultural projects, and global partnerships, LASTHUMAN Foundation works to create sustainable, ethical and inclusive models for the coexistence of people and intelligent machines.
