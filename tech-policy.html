Technology Policy

Influencing international technology policy through research, advocacy and collaboration with policymakers worldwide.

Why This Matters Now

Regulatory Fragmentation

Competing national AI laws create compliance chaos—EU AI Act vs. US sectoral approach vs. China's governance model.

Innovation vs. Protection

Policymakers face impossible choice: stifle breakthrough technologies or expose populations to untested risks.

Geopolitical Stakes

Technology governance determines economic competitiveness, national security, and democratic resilience.

Governance Gap

Legacy institutions lack technical capacity to regulate AI, biotech, and quantum technologies effectively.

Our Focus Areas

  • AI Regulatory Frameworks — Evidence-based legislation balancing innovation with safety and rights
  • Data Governance Models — Sovereignty, cross-border flows, and privacy-preserving architectures
  • Digital Rights Advocacy — Protecting freedoms in algorithmic, platform, and surveillance contexts
  • International Harmonization — Coordinating standards across jurisdictions to prevent fragmentation
  • Emerging Tech Governance — Anticipatory policy for quantum, biotech, and neurotech
  • Regulatory Sandboxes — Safe experimentation zones for policy innovation
  • Institutional Capacity Building — Training regulators in technical evaluation methods
